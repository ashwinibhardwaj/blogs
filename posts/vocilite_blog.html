Vocilite: Simple Text Explanations with Voice

Posted on: July 13, 2025

My Story Behind Vocilite

A few weekends ago, I found myself struggling through a dense article filled with technical jargon. Every couple of sentences, I’d pause, open a new tab, and Google terms just to keep up. That’s when it hit me: there has to be a simpler way to instantly understand complex text without breaking focus.

That moment sparked the idea for Vocilite — a tool designed to help anyone highlight text on any webpage and get a clear, simple explanation right there. And not just visually, but spoken out loud in a natural voice, making it more accessible for all kinds of users.

Vocilite isn’t live yet, but I’ve been building it piece by piece. You can check out the progress and the code on GitHub: Vocilite GitHub Repository.

The Problem I Wanted to Solve

The internet is full of complex articles, research papers, and technical blogs that can be overwhelming. I wanted to solve a few key challenges:

  • Understanding tricky terms or sentences instantly, without switching tabs or losing context.
  • Providing explanations in plain, human language rather than dictionary definitions.
  • Offering audio playback of explanations for people who prefer listening or are multitasking.

My goal was to make understanding text as smooth and fast as possible — whether for students, professionals, or everyday readers.

My Solution: Vocilite

Vocilite is a Chrome extension (still in development) that lets you highlight any text on a webpage and get a simple, conversational explanation powered by AI. It doesn’t stop there — Vocilite can also read the explanation aloud in clear, natural-sounding voices, including options for Indian English accents for better local understanding.

  • Highlight and Explain: Select any text and click “Explain” in the extension panel.
  • Plain English Explanations: The AI generates concise, easy-to-grasp explanations without jargon.
  • Text-to-Speech: Listen to explanations spoken aloud for greater accessibility.
  • AI-Powered: Built using LangChain, Groq’s LLaMA 3, and a Flask backend for fast responses.
  • Clean Audio Output: Vocilite cleans AI text so your voice assistant doesn’t read out symbols or formatting.

How It Works

  1. Step 1: Install the Vocilite Chrome extension (coming soon!).
  2. Step 2: Highlight any text you’d like explained.
  3. Step 3: Click the “Explain” button in Vocilite’s floating panel.
  4. Step 4: See a simple explanation on screen — or listen to it read aloud instantly.

Challenges I Faced

  • Cleaning AI-generated responses to avoid awkward symbols being spoken aloud.
  • Finding high-quality voices for different accents, especially Indian English.
  • Balancing short explanations with enough detail to be genuinely helpful.
  • Designing a UI that feels lightweight and blends seamlessly with web browsing.

What I Learned

  • Integrating LangChain and Groq’s LLaMA 3 to build real-time, user-friendly tools.
  • Building Chrome extensions and managing complex DOM manipulations elegantly.
  • Cleaning and preprocessing AI text for a better speech synthesis experience.
  • Creating tools that bring AI closer to everyday use and improve accessibility.
